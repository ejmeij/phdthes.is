Definitions

Concept
a cognitive unit of meaning that has been agreed upon and formalized in a knowledge structure such as a controlled vocabulary, thesaurus, or ontology.
Concept language
the concepts used to define and describe a knowledge structure, for example, an ontology, thesaurus, or controlled vocabulary.
Conceptual query model
a conceptual model of a query, i.e., a distribution over concepts relevant to a query.
Generative concept model
a language model over vocabulary terms associated with a concept.
Conceptual mapping
an unweighted mapping between queries and concepts.
